About

Young Jae Lee

Independent researcher · Daegu, Republic of Korea

Young Jae Lee is an independent researcher working outside institutional affiliation on the Volume Particle (VP) framework — a model of the vacuum and of continuous media as a jammed packing of finite-volume quanta — and its consequences across physics, fluid dynamics, cosmology, geodynamics, and the life and earth sciences.

The work is organised as a single research program held to one standard: every quantitative claim is derived and reproducible, graded for how much is established, and deposited with its code and data under a DOI. The aim is reproducibility and falsifiability — the results are offered as rigorous, alternative interpretations, not as established replacements for the standard accounts.
