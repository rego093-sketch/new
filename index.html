One premise, many scales

Volume Particle (VP) theory treats space not as a smooth backdrop but as a fully packed, jammed arrangement of identical rigid quanta whose only freedom is rotation. Light is the elastic wave of this lattice, so its speed emerges as c² = B/ρ rather than being postulated. The same packing, depleted by matter that annihilates quanta, produces gravity as an inflow; coarse-grained, it produces the balance laws of fluids; unjammed, it produces a failure mode for rock. The physics whitepaper is the foundation; the other volumes are its consequences.

Every quantitative claim follows a LOCK → Derive → Gate specification, is graded (forced / calibrated / open, or degenerate / distinguishing / conflicting), and ships reproducible code and data under a DOI. There is no parameter tuning, and the order of work is evidence first, then theory.

How every claim is made

The same protocol runs through all six whitepapers — it is what lets an independent reader re-derive and re-run each result.

LOCK → Derive → Gate

Inputs are locked first, results are derived with no omitted steps, and each must pass a reproducible numerical gate before it stands.

No parameter tuning

Every number is forced by geometry, calibrated against one declared anchor, or marked open. Reproducibility is guaranteed by the deposited record.

Evidence first, then theory

Claims are read off reproducible computations and real data and graded; interpretation beyond what the data force is flagged or avoided.
