Questions

Why must the interaction be three-body?

Under the unique isotropic quadratic-spin kernel a co-rotating pair cannot self-propel (a No-Go theorem), so the minimal interaction is an irreducible three-body term whose smallest unit is a triangle.

What does “configured” mean?

The governing equations, selected lengths, and dissipation are read off the underlying jammed arrangement rather than assumed; the operational, falsifiable form is the RG fixed point where the spatial invariant stops flowing.
