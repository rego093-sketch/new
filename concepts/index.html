How the volumes connect

Six whitepapers, one jammed medium. The same handful of ideas recurs across scales.

Shared concepts

Jamming / Volume Particle (VP)
Space and continuous media as a fully packed, jammed arrangement of identical finite-volume quanta whose only freedom is rotation; small disturbances are elastic waves. Appears in: Physics · Fluid · Geodynamics
Random close packing & the isostatic point
The pack self-organises to φ_jam ≈ 0.64 and passes through z = 2d = 6, where the relaxed shear modulus vanishes while the bulk modulus stays finite. Appears in: Physics
Emergent speed of light, c² = B/ρ
With the shear branch gone at the isostatic point, one longitudinal wave speed survives and is identified with the speed of light. Appears in: Physics · Cosmology · Fluid
Event rate ν_p = 3π⁴ (n-fold law)
A geometric event-rate law ν_n = n·π^(2(n−1)) fixes the electron rate ν_e = 1 and the proton rate ν_p = 3π⁴; it sets the mass ratio and the cosmological inflow. Appears in: Physics · Cosmology
Vacuum inflow
Matter annihilates quanta at a fixed rate, so the medium flows inward; the momentum a body absorbs from that inflow is gravity (1/r²). Appears in: Cosmology · Physics
Dynamic stiffness — the “eggshell” / unjamming law
Rate-dependent stiffness with an unjamming trigger (Ψ_eff > Ψ_y) and a self-healing integrity variable g(t); the same law appears as a granular failure mode and as a biological stiffness shell. Appears in: Physics · Geodynamics · DNA
Thin-shell rigidity
A soft interior plus a structured surface yields rigidity — one principle from a micron condensate to an eggshell to growth-plate mineralisation. Appears in: DNA · Physics
Rotational core-capacity identity
N⋆ r_eff² = φ s L_core² fixes a rotational core's effective length once the arrangement is specified; it recurs in fluid vortices and in genome packing. Appears in: Fluid · DNA
Rearrangement-event dissipation, ε_bind = Θσ
Energy leaves the conservative core only through rearrangement events; the same channel carries fluid dissipation and granular failure. Appears in: Fluid · Geodynamics
LOCK → Derive → Gate
Inputs are locked before use; results are derived only through permitted transformations; each must pass a reproducible numerical gate (PASS / FAIL / INCONCLUSIVE). Appears in: Physics · Fluid · Geodynamics · Cosmology · DNA · Geochronology
